Welcome to Rafishaz Interiors

At Rafishaz Interiors we work with clients to create aesthetically pleasing rooms and spaces. Clients range from homeowners to large corporations, and the spaces designed are equally varied, ranging from simple indoor and outdoor home environments to Offices, Hotel lobbies and lavish mansions. Rafishaz Interiors, we specialize in designing and building interior spaces for commercial, retail, hospitality and residential sectors amongst other major and minor renovations. Our emphasis on detail and passion for quality material at competitive prices provides a complete, tailored service covering each and every aspect of any project’s requirements.

GOALS AND OBJECTIVES.

Rafishaz Interiors works to create spaces that are attractive, functional, and safe while meeting the specific needs of our clients. While working with our clients, we strive to achieve our goals and objectives through: -
-Communicating with clients to understand their style, wants and budget
-Developing a design plan, blueprints and budget estimate.
-Working with building inspectors to ensure plans are up to code.
-Hiring architects and contractors to complete structural, construction and remodeling needs.

Our Services @Rafishaz Interiors

Interior design consultancy.

Rafishaz Interiors offers expert guidance and planning for residential or commercial spaces, helping clients envision and create functional and aesthetically pleasing interiors.

Interior construction.

At Rafishaz Interiors we offer high quality interior construction finishes and excellent interpretation of interior designers and architects drawings.

Kitchen Design & Installations.

Kitchens, Wardrobes and Re-modelling. Select from our huge range of kitchen finishes and experiences to create a space in tandem with your lifestyle.

Office partitions/fit-out

These fit-outs can transform spaces into more functional and aesthetically pleasing work environments.

Gypsum design.

Gypsum ceilings are fast becoming one of the most popular choices for new build projects and also with homeowners looking to remodel or renovate a space.

Floor treatment.

Floor treatment services encompass a range of options to clean, repair, and enhance different types of flooring, including cleaning, sanding, polishing, and sealing. These services can be tailored to specific flooring materials like wood, tile, vinyl, and carpet.

Landscaping.

We are a professional landscaping company dedicated to providing top-notch landscaping services to enhance the beauty and value of your outdoor spaces.
